Global component sales returned to year-over-year growth, driven by record third quarter sales in Asia.
With us on the call today are Mike Long, Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer; Chris Stansbury, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer; Andy King, President, Global Components; and Sean Kerins, President, Global Enterprise Computing Solutions.
Enterprise computing solutions sales of $1.92 billion decreased 7% year-over-year on a non-GAAP basis, but were near the high end of our prior expected range.
