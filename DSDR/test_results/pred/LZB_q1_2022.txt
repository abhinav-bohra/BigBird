Compared with the pre-pandemic fiscal '20, first quarter, sales increased 27% for a compounded annual growth rate of 13%, reflecting strong execution at the store level.
Our effective tax rate on a GAAP basis for the fiscal '22 first quarter was 25.9% versus 19.8% in the first quarter of fiscal 2021.
Non-GAAP operating margin for the wholesale segment was 4.7%, reflecting expected gross margin pressure as raw material and freight cost increases outpaced the realization of previously announced pricing actions and we continue to invest in bringing online new capacity.
Earlier this week, demonstrating its confidence in the Company's ability to grow profitably and continue to generate strong cash flow from operations, the Board of Directors approved an increase of 6.5 million shares to the Company's existing share repurchase authorization, with a 2.5 million shares available for repurchase under the program as of the end of the fiscal 2022 first quarter.
Demand across all businesses also remained high and backlog remains at record levels.
We also paid $7 million in dividends to shareholders in the last quarter.
