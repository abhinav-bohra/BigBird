Digital money transfer revenues, which include wu.com and digital partnerships increased 15% on a reported basis or 14% constant currency.
Total C2C cross-border principal increased 4% on a reported basis or 3% constant currency, driven by growth in digital money transfer.
We now expect full year 2021 GAAP revenue growth will be approximately 150 basis points higher than constant currency revenue growth.
Overall, we achieved revenue growth in the quarter of 2% on a reported and constant currency basis, which was driven by 15% growth in digital and 31% growth in Business Solutions.
Business Solutions revenue increased 31% on a reported basis or 28% constant currency.
The spread between C2C transaction and revenue growth was one percentage point on a reported basis and flat on a constant currency basis.
As a result, our C2C revenue was flat on a reported basis or down 1% in constant currency terms with transaction growth down 1%.
The driver of the spread between transactions and constant currency revenue growth was due to business mix.
North America revenue decreased 2% on both a reported and constant currency basis, on transaction declines of 5%.
Constant currency revenue declines were driven by the regional business.
