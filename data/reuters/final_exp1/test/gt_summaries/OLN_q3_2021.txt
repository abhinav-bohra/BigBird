OLIN ANNOUNCES THIRD QUARTER 2021 RESULTS.
Q3 EARNINGS PER SHARE $2.38.
Q3 SALES $2.34 BILLION.
QTRLY ADJUSTED EBITDA ACHIEVED DESPITE PRODUCTION INTERRUPTIONS FROM HURRICANES, RAW MATERIAL COST VOLATILITY, & SUPPLY-CHAIN DISRUPTIONS.

EXPECTS Q4 2021 ADJUSTED EBITDA TO BE COMPARABLE TO OR SLIGHTLY LOWER THAN Q3 2021 LEVELS.
