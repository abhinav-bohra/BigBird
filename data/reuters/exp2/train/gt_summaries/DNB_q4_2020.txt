Dun & Bradstreet Sees FY 2021 Adjusted EBITDA In Range Of $840 Million To $855 Million.
DUN & BRADSTREET REPORTS FOURTH QUARTER AND FULL YEAR 2020 FINANCIAL RESULTS.
Q4 ADJUSTED EARNINGS PER SHARE $0.28.
Q4 LOSS PER SHARE $0.02.
SEES FY 2021 ADJUSTED EARNINGS PER SHARE $1.02 TO $1.06.
REVENUE FOR Q4 OF 2020 WAS $480.1 MILLION, AN INCREASE OF 11.0% AS REPORTED, AND 10.5% ON A CONSTANT CURRENCY BASIS.
FULL YEAR 2021 ADJUSTED REVENUES ARE EXPECTED TO BE IN RANGE OF $2,145 MILLION TO $2,175 MILLION.
FULL YEAR 2021 ADJUSTED EBITDA IS EXPECTED TO BE IN RANGE OF $840 MILLION TO $855 MILLION.
