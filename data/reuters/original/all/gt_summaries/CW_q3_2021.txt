Curtiss-Wright Reports Strong Third Quarter 2021 Results.

CURTISS-WRIGHT REPORTS STRONG THIRD QUARTER 2021 RESULTS AND RAISES MIDPOINT OF FULL-YEAR 2021 ADJUSTED EPS GUIDANCE.
Q3 ADJUSTED EARNINGS PER SHARE $1.88.
Q3 EARNINGS PER SHARE $1.70.
QTRLY ADJUSTED SALES OF $614 MILLION, UP 12%.
QTRLY NEW ORDERS OF $617 MILLION, UP 13%.
RAISED BOTTOM END OF FULL-YEAR 2021  ADJUSTED DILUTED EPS GUIDANCE TO NEW RANGE OF $7.20 TO $7.35 (PREVIOUSLY $7.15 TO $7.35).
MAINTAINED FULL-YEAR 2021 SALES GROWTH OF 7% TO 9%.
Q3 EARNINGS PER SHARE VIEW $1.82, REVENUE VIEW $609.4 MILLION -- REFINITIV IBES DATA.
FY2021 EARNINGS PER SHARE VIEW $7.22, REVENUE VIEW $2.50 BILLION -- REFINITIV IBES DATA.
