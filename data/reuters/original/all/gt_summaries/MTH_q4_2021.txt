Meritage Homes Reports Q4 2021 Results Including 500 Bps Increase In Home Closing Gross Margin.

MERITAGE HOMES REPORTS FOURTH QUARTER 2021 RESULTS INCLUDING 500 BPS INCREASE IN HOME CLOSING GROSS MARGIN, 33% INCREASE IN YEAR-END COMMUNITY COUNT TO 259 AND 57% INCREASE IN DILUTED EPS OVER PRIOR YEAR.
Q4 EARNINGS PER SHARE $6.25.
QTRLY HOMES CLOSED 3,526 VERSUS 3,744.
QTRLY SALES ORDERS OF 3,367 HOMES WERE 6% HIGHER THAN PRIOR YEAR.
QTRLY TOTAL CLOSING REVENUE $1.5 BILLION VERSUS $1.41 BILLION.
Q4 EARNINGS PER SHARE VIEW $6.03, REVENUE VIEW $1.51 BILLION -- REFINITIV IBES DATA.
EXPECT TO CONTINUE TO BENEFIT FROM INCREMENTAL ORDERS VOLUME AND CLOSINGS IN 2022 AND BEYOND.
